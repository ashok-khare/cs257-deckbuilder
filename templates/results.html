Interpreting Your Results

The results of your search are laid out as follows. The cards contained in the deck you searched for are displayed first, then the statistics of the deck that you chose to view are listed below that.

There are four statistics that may be listed below:

  • Elixir Cost: The average elixir cost of the cards in this deck.
  • Win Rate: The win rate of this deck, as a percentage.
  • Average User Trophy Count: The average trophy count of the users that use this deck.
  • Number of Wins: The number of matches in which the player using this deck won.
